Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Kande VendTech ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at kandevendtech.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

Information We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily provide it. This happens when you:

  • Submit a contact or quote request form on our website
  • Send us an email directly
  • Call or text us

The information you provide may include your name, email address, phone number, business name, and the details of your inquiry.

We also collect standard website analytics data automatically through Google Analytics (GA4), including pages visited, time on site, browser type, and general geographic location. This data is aggregated and not linked to individuals.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or request
  • Schedule consultations and service installations
  • Send you follow-up communications related to your request
  • Improve our website and services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics places cookies on your device to collect anonymous usage data. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Our contact forms are processed by FormSubmit.co, which transmits form submissions to our email. FormSubmit.co does not store your data beyond what is needed to deliver the message.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and email correspondence for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain records of our business relationship. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@kandevendtech.com.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for business use and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
