Vending That Actually Works for Schools

Schools have different vending needs than offices or hotels. K-12 schools need products that meet USDA Smart Snacks standards. Universities want variety, from late-night study fuel to grab-and-go lunch between classes. Teacher lounges need coffee and real snacks. And administrators don't want one more thing to manage. We work with school administrators and campus facilities teams to build vending programs that fit nutrition policies and student preferences. For K-12, we stock compliant products that students will actually buy. No more machines full of healthy items that collect dust. Our AI tracks what sells and swaps out what doesn't. For universities and community colleges across Las Vegas, we offer snack machines, drink machines, coffee machines, and fresh meal smart coolers in dorms, student unions, libraries, rec centers, and faculty buildings. Everything is free to the school. We handle the machines, the products, the restocking, and the maintenance. Revenue sharing is available for busy campus locations.
